Takeaways

Designing in healthcare required balancing clarity, empathy, and precision within complex technical and regulatory constraints. Across the module experiences I worked on, I focused on translating dense clinical data into interfaces that felt clear, scannable, and actionable for patients and caregivers.

This work reinforced the importance of early data understanding, close collaboration with engineers and researchers, and iterative testing to uncover what information truly matters to users, especially in moments that are often stressful or high-stakes.

I'm extremely grateful for the engineers, PMs, and researchers I worked with on this project and learned so much about design systems, user research, and healthcare-specific product nuances that I'm excited to take forward!
