Overview

Designing a personalized patient portal for managing care.

Our client was a leading US healthcare provider building its own patient portal/app to enhance patient engagement and access to services. How can we build a seamless hospital app that empowers patients through personalized messaging and comprehensive data compilation, delivering an intuitive and unified digital experience that enhances communication, streamlines care coordination, and supports informed health decisions at every step of the patient journey?


Timeline

After onboarding, I took ownership of the Vaccinations and Visit Summaries modules, driving design improvements and feature development.




Vaccinations exploration & intial stages

In the early prototyping phase of the Vaccinations module, I explored how vaccination data is structured‚Äîwhat's required, how completion and dates are tracked, and how to clearly surface status to users. With COVID-19 still recent, we also considered no-contact ways to verify vaccination, especially for COVID shots. This was a highly exploratory phase, focused on understanding data flow and usability, resulting in a set of low-fidelity prototypes to test core display and interaction ideas.

After four rounds of user testing, we uncovered key insights that shaped the direction of the Vaccinations module: 1) Sharing mattered most: Parents especially valued the ability to send their child's immunization records to schools, camps, and healthcare providers. 2) COVID stood apart: Users had different expectations for COVID vaccination records compared to routine vaccines, often prioritizing no-contact sharing and quick verification. 3) Email sharing was preferred: Participants found emailing records to third parties simple and useful. These findings pointed to clear next steps: ‚Üí Deepen research around Download and Share workflows ‚Üí Explore integration with Messaging for seamless communication

Research + Surveying

Learning from the expertise of the team's UX Researcher and receiving guidance from my mentor, I conducted research with business users and developers. Setting up user testing was an insightful experience where I learned how to drill down on necessary information to improve the homepage experience.


Final Thoughts

At the end of the day, users want a comprehensive yet uncomplicated dashboard to visualize pertinent information. As an intern and someone with a fresh set of eyes, I needed to understand UiPath's product ecosystem and discern what was important from a new user's perspective. I loved the UiPath culture (even though I had a virtual internship experience), gained the best mentorship, and learned how to conduct effective user testing. It was a great summer!
