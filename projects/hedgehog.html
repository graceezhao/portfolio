Overview

Building a cohesive brand identity for a high-growth AgTech startup.

Hedgehog is revolutionizing agricultural operations through automation. How can we design a visual identity that communicates innovation and reliability while appealing to modern farmers and investors?


Timeline

Working closely with the founders, I moved from initial brand discovery to full visual identity design, website development in Webflow, and social media execution.




Branding & Strategy

The branding process began with identifying Hedgehog's core values: resilience, efficiency, and technological advancement. We needed a brand that felt grounded in agriculture yet forward-thinking. I conducted market research on AgTech competitors to find a unique positioning.

After several iterations and stakeholder feedback, I established a design system that prioritized:

1) Modern Typography and
2) Earth-toned Palettes with Tech Accents.

This foundation allowed us to scale the brand across physical packaging and digital platforms consistently.




Website Design


Initial wireframes focused on clear communication of the value proposition.

Final Webflow implementation with custom animations and responsive layouts.


Final Deliverables


Takeaways:

1) Simplicity in messaging is key for B2B AgTech
2) Mobile-first design is essential for on-field utility
3) Iterative feedback from the field improves design relevance


Growth & Operations

Beyond design, I managed social media strategy and operational workflows. By automating content distribution and leveraging analytics, we achieved a 10x growth in follower count and established a consistent 3,000+ monthly reach.


Final Thoughts

Building Hedgehog from its early stages was an incredible journey in agility and design thinking. Working in a Seed stage environment required wearing many hats and balancing speed with quality.
