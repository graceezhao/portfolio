My summer working on a healthcare case at BCG Platinion, the firm’s tech build and design division.

Experience Design Intern

BCG Platinion envisions and designs transformative digital solutions.

Working as a design consultant provided a completely different experience compared to being an in-house designer. The best part of the internship was the opportunity to closely interact with the client and work on the broader scope of the project.

I loved my experience working on the case, even though healthcare was a relatively new industry to me. Working with talented & knowledgeable designers, researchers, engineers, and consultants was the highlight of my experience at the firm. Throughout my time as an intern, I thoroughly enjoyed seeing others’ work as well as presenting and hearing feedback for my own work.

Thank you to the support I got from everyone on the case team as well as in the SF & NYC offices. Special shoutout to the designers who welcomed me both in person and virtually. Grateful to be a part of the BCG community!
